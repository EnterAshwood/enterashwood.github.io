About the Author

Dale writes dark fantasy that feels cinematic, immersive, and just a little too real once you put the book down.

Dale is a dark-fantasy storyteller obsessed with atmosphere—haunted forests, old magic, and the quiet moments where characters decide who they really are. His stories blend visual, almost film-like scenes with emotional weight, building worlds that feel lived-in and dangerous.

When he’s not writing, he’s experimenting with creative tools, designing visuals, and shaping the Ashwood universe into something readers can step into, not just read about.

“Fantasy should feel like a place you could walk into—beautiful, unsettling, and impossible to forget once you leave.”
